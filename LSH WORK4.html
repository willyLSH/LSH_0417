Exploration #01

The

Creature

that Devours Sunsets

The theme of 'the monster that eats sunsets' was chosen because I experienced a series of thrilling adventures at the beach that day. First, my pet guinea pig, 3 Layers , was attacked by an eagle, which was truly spectacular but also terrifying. It left me feeling as hopeless as the sunset that day, and I realized just how terrifying eagles can be. If you want to know more about the story of how 3 Layers was caught by the eagle, you can contact me to find out. Finally, when we were leaving, I was also attacked by the eagle. Although I wasn't seriously injured, it was still a stroke of luck. Adventure is always full of surprisesÔºÅ
